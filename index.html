About

Vincent Willem Van Gogh was born in Zundert - Netherlands at March 30, 1853. He was a post-impressionist painter, considered one of the most famous and influente figure of the Western Art Histoy. He created more than two thousand works during more than ten years, including 860 oil paintings. His artworks are about sights, dead nature, portraits and self-portraits. He used to use dramatic and vibrant colors, in addition impulsive and expressive brushstrokes that contribuited to mordern art fundation and brought distiction to the painter style.
